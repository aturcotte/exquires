Creation of the test image from the Library of Congress scan, using NIP2:
-Crop left 1000, top 400, width 4200, height 4200
-Import with built-in sRGB Perceptual
-Convert to XYZ
-im_shrink 5 5 (5x5 box downsampling to 840x840)
-Export with sRGB_IEC61966-2-1_black_scaled.icc (downloaded from
 http://www.color.org/srgbprofiles.xalter)
-Save as TIFF with Zip (deflate) with Horizontal difference predictor

Finally, the file was stripped with exiftool -overwrite_original -all=
